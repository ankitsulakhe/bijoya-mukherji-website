About Me

Hi, I'm Bijoya —
your guide home.

I am a Nervous System-led Emotional Mastery Coach, and I specialise in personal transformations with over six years of experience supporting high-achieving individuals in creating sustainable success from a regulated, grounded state. I am a certified Pranic Energy Healer, EFT Coach, Crystal Reiki Practitioner and hold an international certification in Yoga and Breathwork, which, along with self-awareness tools, forms the foundation of my work.

Before this, I was heading design teams in high-pressure corporate environments, consistently working toward ambitious targets. While I was achieving externally, I was also experiencing deep physical, mental, and emotional burnout. That contrast led me inward, to explore and understand the root causes of stress, performance, and human behaviour at a deeper level.

I am still deeply ambitious. I create, lead, and aim high. That’s exactly why I do this work. I support individuals who want to achieve and build without sacrificing their health, relationships, or inner stability. My approach goes beyond surface-level tools, rooted in somatic work, cognitive re-patterning, nervous system regulation, and immersive energy and self-reflection practices.

With support and structure, my clients are guided to move out of chronic stress and survival patterns into clarity, balance, and aligned success, thriving both professionally and personally, where ambition feels expansive, not exhausting, and success is something they can truly sustain.

Our Process

Your Transformation Journey

A gentle, body-forward path back to yourself - through four deeply nourishing stages of healing.

01

Awareness

We shift from thinking about the body to witnessing it as one integrated system. Noticing the subtle responses, and the way your body continuously receives and reflects your inner state.

02

Regulation

Using somatic techniques, breathwork, and nervous system education, we cultivate a felt sense of safety — the foundation of all lasting change.

03

Release

With Energy Healing, Somatic & Cognitive tools, old emotions, stored tension, and limiting beliefs are gently welcomed and discharged from the body — not forced, but invited to complete their cycle.

04

Integration

New patterns are embodied and anchored into daily life. This is where transformation becomes lived experience — sustainable, grounded, and lasting.
