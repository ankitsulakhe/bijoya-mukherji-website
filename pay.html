Common Questions
How do sessions work? ▼
All sessions are held online via video call — from the comfort of your own home. After booking, you'll receive a welcome email with a scheduling link to choose your preferred time slot within 48 hours.
Do I need any prior experience? ▼
Not at all. My work is designed for everyone — whether you're new to somatic healing or have done therapy before. We always start exactly where you are, with no pressure or expectations.
What if I need to reschedule? ▼
Life happens. You can reschedule any session with at least 24 hours' notice at no charge. Just drop me a WhatsApp message and we'll find a time that works for you.
Is payment secure? ▼
Yes — all payments are processed through Razorpay, India's most trusted payment gateway. Your card details are never stored and all transactions are encrypted with bank-grade security.
Can I get a refund? ▼
Single sessions are non-refundable after confirmation, but can be rescheduled. For packages, if you feel the work isn't right for you after the first session, I offer a partial refund — your wellbeing matters more than a transaction.
